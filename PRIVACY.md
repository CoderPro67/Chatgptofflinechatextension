# Privacy Policy - ChatGPT Offline Archive

## Data Collection
This extension does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Local Storage Only
- All ChatGPT conversations are stored locally on your device
- No data is sent to external servers or third parties
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry

## Permissions Used
- **activeTab**: Access current ChatGPT tab to save conversations
- **storage**: Store conversations locally in browser storage
- **scripting**: Inject content script to detect messages
- **tabs**: Open offline viewer in new tab

## Data Security
- All data remains on your device
- Uses browser's built-in storage encryption
- No network requests made by the extension
